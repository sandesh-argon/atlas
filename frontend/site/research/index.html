Anchor Findings

Four mechanism classes. Four different policy failures to avoid.

Reversal

The Same Policy Can Help or Hurt

The education-fertility link reverses by development stratum. Negative in developing and emerging economies, positive in advanced. Active in all 140 graphs, all 35 years.

Read finding F02 →
Threshold

Governance Gains Aren't Linear

Local government power shows threshold dynamics at score 3.0 for election quality. Below threshold, larger marginal gains. 66 countries currently below, 108 above.

Read finding F06 →
Mediation

The Direct Path Doesn't Exist

Spending has zero direct effect on agricultural income distribution (0/140 graphs). But route it through fiscal income, and the path appears in all 140 graphs, all 35 years.

Read finding F08 →
Stable Upstream

One Relationship That Doesn't Break

Government benefits to accumulated income: beta ~0.98 across all strata, all decades. The most stable edge in the corpus. A benchmark for scenario calibration.
